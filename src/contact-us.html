Installation

Extremely Simple Installation - Fits Right Into Your Vehicle

  • Open your vehicle's hood.
  • Open the back cap of the headlight assembly and take out the stock bulb.
  • Insert the LED bulb into the headlight assembly.
  • Plug the LED bulb to power socket.
  • You're done. Installation is as simple as swapping out the bulbs.

Installation Notes

  • Be very attentive during the installation process to minimize all chances of accidents.
  • Never touch the bulb with your fingers because it might blemish it.
  • Wearing gloves is ideal when dealing with the bulb. If ever it gets in contact with any contaminents, simply wipe the bulb with rubbing alcohol and a dry cloth.
  • Always perform above operation when vehicle is off, not running.
  • Do not touch bulb when turning power on and off.
