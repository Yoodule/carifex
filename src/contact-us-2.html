MY HEADLIGHTS ARE FLICKERING AFTER INSTALLATION

If the bulbs are flickering, below are possible solutions:

  • The bulb may not be bad but we cannot tell unless you test them using a meter reader or Battery charger of 12v to see if they actually get lit up outside the vehicle.
  • If the bulbs come on outside the vehicle and light up very well, then you may need to get a canbus decoder so they stop flickering when plugged into your vehicle (https://carifex.com/products/led-headlight-canbus-wiring-harness-adapter)
  • If they don't come up after the test, then the bulbs are bad and may need replacement from us.
  • Replacement can be done only after the test has been carried out. A photo/video proof is required before emailing us, because if we send in another replacement and the same thing happened that's not good for the both of us.

If the issue is not resolved after this steps, please write back to us for a REFUND procedure.
